## Viral Style Rules

### Hooks Must Be Aggressive And Scroll-Stopping

The first sentence decides everything.

Hooks must:
- Feel like forbidden knowledge  
- Trigger curiosity or fear of missing out  
- Challenge what people believe  
- Make the viewer feel they are about to learn something hidden  

Strong hook angles:
- “Most people have this backwards…”
- “Nobody tells you this…”
- “This was hidden for years…”
- “You’ve been doing this wrong your whole life…”
- “There’s a reason this isn’t talked about…”

Weak hooks (never use):
- “In today’s video…”
- “Have you ever wondered…”
- “Hey guys…”
- Anything slow, polite, or explanatory

---

## Viral Amplification Rules (NEW)

- Every script should feel like something people are not supposed to know  
- Prioritize scroll-stopping over being safe or neutral  
- Use curiosity, tension, or contradiction in every opening  
- Make the viewer feel like they’re missing out on hidden information  
- Scripts should feel fast, intense, and impossible to ignore  

---

## Sentence Style

- Most sentences under 15 words  
- Mix short and slightly longer sentences for rhythm  
- Never stack long sentences  
- Must sound natural when spoken  

---

## Emotional & Curiosity Layer

Every section should:
- Open loops  
- Build tension  
- Make the viewer want the next line  

Use triggers:
- Surprise  
- Curiosity  
- Contradiction  
- Validation  
- Aspiration  

---

## Hidden Knowledge Style

Use framing like:
- “Most people have no idea…”
- “Almost nobody talks about this…”
- “This was buried in research…”
- “There’s a reason this isn’t common knowledge…”

Never sound conspiratorial. Always sound grounded.

---

## Pacing

- New idea every 2–3 sentences  
- No concept longer than ~20 seconds  
- Always build toward a payoff

## Compliance Rules (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### No Medical Claims
- Do not claim anything treats, cures, heals, or diagnoses  
- Do not promise outcomes  
- Do not replace medical advice  

### No Disease References
- Do not mention diseases  
- Do not imply solving medical conditions  

### Banned Words
Never use:
- treat
- cure
- heal
- diagnose

### Approved Language
Use:
- support  
- optimize  
- balance  
- recharge  
- restore  
- reset  
- improve wellbeing  
- enhance performance  

### Outcome Framing
Focus on:
- Energy  
- Focus  
- Clarity  
- Sleep  
- Recovery  
- Performance  
- Wellbeing

## CTA RULE (UPDATED)

Every script must end with a call to action asking viewers to comment “RESONANCE”.

- The wording must be different every time  
- Do not repeat the same sentence  
- Do not just write “RESONANCE” alone  

Example styles:
- “Comment RESONANCE and I’ll show you how this works”
- “Drop RESONANCE below if you want the full breakdown”
- “Type RESONANCE if you want to go deeper into this”

---

## Universal Output Shape

Every script must:

1. Start with a powerful hook  
2. Deliver a fast-paced, curiosity-driven body  
3. End with a varied CTA using “RESONANCE”  
4. Be plain text only  
5. Have no formatting, labels, or structure markers
